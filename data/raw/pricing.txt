BRIGHT SMILE DENTAL STUDIO — PRICING & FEES

Last updated: March 2025. Fees are estimates in USD and may vary by case complexity, materials chosen, and insurance allowances. A written treatment estimate is always provided before elective work begins.

NEW PATIENT PACKAGE
• Adult comprehensive exam + full-mouth X-rays + cleaning (if clinically appropriate): $285–$350
• Child exam (under 14) + X-rays + cleaning: $195–$240

ROUTINE PREVENTIVE (ESTABLISHED PATIENTS)
• Periodic exam: $75–$95
• Adult prophylaxis (cleaning): $120–$150
• Periodontal maintenance (after gum therapy): $165–$195
• Bitewing X-rays (set of four): $45–$60
• Panoramic X-ray: $95–$125

RESTORATIVE (COMMON PROCEDURES)
• Composite filling (one surface): $165–$220
• Composite filling (two surfaces): $220–$295
• Porcelain crown: $1,150–$1,450
• Core buildup (when needed under crown): $225–$325

COSMETIC
• In-office whitening (full arch): $495–$650
• Custom take-home whitening trays + gel: $325–$425
• Porcelain veneer (per tooth): $1,200–$1,600

SURGICAL / ENDO
• Simple extraction: $185–$275
• Non-surgical root canal (anterior): $850–$1,050
• Non-surgical root canal (molar): $1,100–$1,400

IMPLANTS (BALLPARK—INCLUDES PLANNING)
• Single implant, crown, and components (typical case): $3,800–$4,800 total before insurance

PAYMENT
We accept cash, major credit and debit cards, HSA/FSA cards, and CareCredit subject to approval. Payment is due at the time of service unless a formal payment plan has been arranged for larger treatment plans.

INSURANCE
We are in-network with several PPO plans; benefits vary. Our team will submit claims on your behalf and help you understand estimated out-of-pocket costs—not a guarantee of payment from your carrier.
