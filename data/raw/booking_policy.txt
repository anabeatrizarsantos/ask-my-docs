BRIGHT SMILE DENTAL STUDIO — BOOKING & CANCELLATION POLICY

HOW TO BOOK
New patients may schedule online at brightsmile-riverdale.com/book or by calling (555) 014-7720. Existing patients can use the patient portal, email, or phone. For orthodontic or implant consultations, please mention it when booking so we allocate the correct room and time.

APPOINTMENT LENGTH
Routine visits are typically 45–60 minutes. Complex treatment or multiple procedures may require longer blocks; we will tell you what to expect when we confirm.

ARRIVAL TIME
Please arrive 10–15 minutes early if you have paperwork to update or if this is your first visit. Late arrival may shorten your appointment or require rescheduling if the clinical team cannot complete treatment safely in the remaining time.

CANCELLATION & NO-SHOW
We respect your time and ask the same in return. If you need to cancel or reschedule, please give us at least 24 hours’ notice (48 hours is appreciated for longer restorative visits). Missed appointments or repeated late cancellations may incur a $75 fee and may require a deposit to reserve future visits.

DEPOSITS
For procedures over $1,000 or lab-fabricated work (crowns, veneers, dentures), a non-refundable deposit may be collected to secure materials and lab time. The amount and terms appear on your treatment plan.

MINORS
Patients under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian for consent and to discuss treatment options, unless prior written authorization is on file.

INSURANCE VERIFICATION
We verify benefits as a courtesy when you provide accurate insurance information at least 48 hours before your visit. Verification is not a guarantee of payment; final responsibility rests with the patient per the Explanation of Benefits from your carrier.

QUESTIONS?
Our coordinators are happy to walk you through scheduling, deposits, or paperwork—just ask.
