BRIGHT SMILE DENTAL STUDIO — ABOUT US

Welcome to Bright Smile Dental Studio, a modern family-oriented practice in the heart of Riverdale. Since 2014 we have combined evidence-based dentistry with a calm, welcoming atmosphere—because we believe oral health should feel approachable, not intimidating.

Our philosophy is simple: listen first, explain clearly, and treat conservatively when it is safe to do so. Every patient receives a personalized care plan that balances clinical excellence with your lifestyle, budget, and long-term goals.

Meet the team. Dr. Elena Vasquez leads our clinical team with a focus on minimally invasive restorative work and cosmetic dentistry. Dr. James Okonkwo specializes in periodontics and implant-supported restorations. Our hygienists, Ana and Marcus, are certified in gentle periodontal therapy and pediatric preventive care. Behind the scenes, our coordinators ensure scheduling, insurance questions, and follow-ups run smoothly.

We invest in technology that matters: digital radiography (lower radiation), intraoral scanning for crowns and aligners, and chairside education screens so you can see what we see. Our sterilization protocols follow CDC and ADA guidelines, and we welcome questions about how we keep you safe.

Bright Smile is proud to support local schools with free dental screenings each spring and to partner with Riverdale Food Bank for oral hygiene kits. Healthy smiles extend beyond our walls—and we are here for the long run.

We look forward to meeting you.

— The Bright Smile Dental Studio team
