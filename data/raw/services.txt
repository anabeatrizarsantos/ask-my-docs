BRIGHT SMILE DENTAL STUDIO — SERVICES

PREVENTIVE CARE
• Comprehensive new-patient exams and periodic checkups
• Professional cleanings (prophylaxis) and periodontal maintenance
• Fluoride varnish and dental sealants for children and high-cavity-risk adults
• Oral cancer screening and TMJ / bite assessment
• Custom night guards for bruxism and sports mouthguards

RESTORATIVE DENTISTRY
• Tooth-colored composite fillings
• Inlays, onlays, and full-coverage crowns (porcelain and zirconia options)
• Bridges and partial dentures; full dentures with optional implant stabilization

COSMETIC DENTISTRY
• Professional in-office and take-home teeth whitening
• Porcelain veneers and cosmetic bonding
• Smile design consultations with digital preview when appropriate

ENDODONTICS & SURGERY
• Root canal therapy with rotary instrumentation
• Simple extractions; referral network for complex oral surgery and IV sedation

ORTHODONTICS & ALIGNERS
• Clear aligner therapy for mild to moderate crowding (case-dependent assessment)
• Retainers and orthodontic retention checks for patients finishing treatment elsewhere

IMPLANTS & PERIODONTAL CARE
• Single and multiple dental implants (planning, placement, and restoration coordination)
• Scaling and root planing; periodontal maintenance programs
• Soft-tissue grafting referrals when indicated

EMERGENCY CARE
• Same-day appointments for acute pain, swelling, or dental trauma during business hours
• After-hours guidance line for established patients (voicemail triage; emergency referrals when needed)

PEDIATRIC-FRIENDLY
• We treat children from age three and up; younger children may be referred to a pediatric specialist if extensive care is required.
